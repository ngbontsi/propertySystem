Exclusive Networking Database For: Property Managers, Landlords and Real Estate Agents


Tenant Database Namibia was started to aid in the leasing decisions of landlords, property managers and apartment complex owners. Tenant Database Namibia is the only website specifically designed for landlords, property managers and apartment complex managers to report and search evictions as they happen in real time.

We are a nationwide database of tenants at all stages of the eviction process: before, during, and post trial. We are not a credit reporting agency, but a private database for landlords, property managers and apartment complex managers.
